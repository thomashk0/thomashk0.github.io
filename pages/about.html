About

Hi, my name is Thomas Hiscock. I am an engineer/researcher in the field of embedded system security. I am working at CEA-Leti (Grenoble, France) since 2014. A non-exhaustive list of my current and past activities includes:

  • Design and implementation of security extensions for processors (MIPS during my PhD and now RISC-V).
  • Maintenance of the side-channel infrastructure of my lab.
  • Many research activities around side-channel analysis.
  • Risk analysis and penetration testing on products with industrials partners.
  • Scripting all the things, especially repetitive administrative tasks!

When I am not working, you are likely to find me on a bike in Grenoble streets, running on trails in neighbouring mountains or skating in winter.
